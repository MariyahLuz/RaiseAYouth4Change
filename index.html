Youth for change Uganda is a non profit organization for youth, working with the grassroots in identifying problems, creating solutions for the problems, and implementation of activities that will help the youth to improve their heath and wellbeing, while advocatating for climate mitigation, and social economic development among the young people.

Community development and creating conditions for integration, equal access and inclusion through expertise and innovative solutions, through supporting youth initiatives, as well as capacity building among the youth to enable them make informed decisions for better lives.

Core Values--

Youth driven

The organization focuses on the youths in all its operations, aiming at making the society a better place from the grassroots.

Belief in young people

We believe that when young people are empowered, they can make a great impact on changing the society positively.

Integrity

We ensure honesty,consistency, and uncompromising adherence to strong moral and ethical principles and values in our work.

Teamwork

Our team is collaborative, friendly and easy to work with hence ensuring that results are deliberate in places of operation.

Loyalty

We are in all our operation, showing firm and constant support or allegiance in every possible way.

Transparency

Being a non profit organization that is funded by individuals, companies and govt entities, we ensure transparency for efficiency in working with more funders.
